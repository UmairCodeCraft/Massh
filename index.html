What are the headings for a strategic plan?

Strategic planning is a process in which an organization's leaders define their vision for the future and identify their organization's goals and objectives. The process includes establishing the sequence in which those goals should be realized so that the organization can reach its stated vision.

What is a time efficient work plan?

Time efficiency is being able to produce desired results with the least amount of wasted resources. So, being time efficient at work means being as productive as possible during your workday while minimizing wasted time, energy, and wellbeing (your most valuable resources!). In simple words, time management is the process of organizing and planning the amount of time you want to divide between specific activities.
